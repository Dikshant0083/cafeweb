About Us

The Moon-light is committed to providing fresh, healthy, and delicious food options. Our ingredients are locally sourced, and our menu is designed to cater to various dietary needs and preferences. At Moonlight, we believe that great coffee and food can brighten any hour of the day. Our carefully curated menu features: Artisanal coffee and espresso drinks A wide selection of teas Fresh pastries and baked goods Light breakfast and lunch options Evening small plates and desserts We source our coffee beans from responsible, sustainable farms and work with local suppliers to bring you the freshest ingredients possible.
